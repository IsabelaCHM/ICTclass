Funny Story

I was with my family hiking in a mountain in the middle of nowhere. My cousin and I were walking in the front until we came across a fence and had to stop. The guide came and opened the fence that was full of cows and told us to go ahead. My cousin and I walked in and waited for the rest of the family on one of the small hills. The cows were around and there was one on another small hill staring deeply at us and liking itself. We turned around to help my grandmother and suddenly we heard hooves clashing against the mud. I turned around and saw the cow hurrying towards my cousin and me. When I tell you that I jumped extremely high is basically me being scared, jumping out of the atmosphere, bouncing in Mars, turning three times in Saturn and flying right back onto Earth just to see the cow on the other side looking like it wanted to chase us again. My cousin and I fled the place and didn’t even wait for the fence to be opened, we jumped over it! I swear my family will never get over it, they will forever laugh.
